Brief

The goal of the project is to build a medium-scale web application (i.e. one that one person alone could not build) that solves a practical problem based on a given brief. In 2018, we want to tackle big societal challenges. The goal is to build a web-based application that contributes towards a solution to one of the challenges proposed by OpenIDEO. Marks Breakdown

The handbook states that the project is worth 50% of the final mark. In previous editions of the course, students suggested that the project should be worth a higher percentage, given its large scope. I agree with this proposal, so, this semester, I will make the project is worth 60% of your final mark. If you wish to be marked according to the handbook, you should let me know before the start of Week 3, otherwise your mark will be automatically calculated with a 60/40 split.

To ensure continuous progress and to maximise the feedback that you receive throughout the semester, the project will be broken down into multiple deliverables. Apart from Weeks 1 and 8, all weeks will have a deliverable due, some for formative assessment and some for summative assessment. This means that even though all will be graded, the mark you will receive for the deliverable will only count towards your final mark in the summative assessment.
